What is An-Ni-Go?

An-Ni-Go is a remake of the Yu-Gi-Oh! card game that originally came from the anime. The main difference between the Yu-Gi-Oh! card game and the An-Ni-Go! card game is that instead of using monsters like in the Yu-Gi-Oh! version to fight, the player now uses anime characters from several different animes to fight instead.

How to play

Playing An-Ni-Go! is very simple, the removal of defenses and trap- and magic cards has made this game easily understood by people of all ages.
1. You decide who plays first, you or the computer. If you decide to play first, you can add a card to the field first, but cannot attack. If you play second, then you can add a card to the field and attack the other players card if you want.
2. If you plan to destroy the other players card, your card must be really strong, it's attack should be higher than your opponents attack.
3. If you succeed in destroying one of his cards he losses HP, the amount he losses can be calculated like this: Your cards attack - His cards attack = damage taken. Remember, this also works the other way around, so you can also get damage when he decides to attack you.
4. If one of you don't have an card on the field when you are attacking each other, the one without a card on the field will take direct damage and will suffer all the attack power in damage...
